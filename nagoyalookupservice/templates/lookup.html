If the lookup results in an empty list please be aware this generally means there are no countries identified within the searched radius. However, there is a small probability
of it being a false negative, i.e. an empty list even though there are countries within the search radius. This might happen if the database is not entirely synchronized with the
legal regulations on the exact EEZ borders of every country, or in case of deviations between EEZ borders of sea floor and water mass. As reliable
the database may be, a search radius of at least 5 km is always recommended to yield an output as concise as desired and as expansive as necessary. If you have profound
reason to expect a country where an empty list is returned we advise you to contact the corresponding NFP. Please also check the United Nations Treaty Collection Website
for any country-specific agreements and declarations that may apply to your searched area.
